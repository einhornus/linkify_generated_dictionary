Одно́ из двух: и́ли увези́ он её, энергически по́ступи, и́ли дай разво́д.
One of two things must happen: either he must take her away, acting energetically – or he must divorce her.

Do therefore according to thy wisdom, and let not his hoar head go down to the grave in peace.
